How it works

What is genCounter?

  • A webapp to help researchers use the gendered language dictionary developed by Roberts and Utych (2019).

How does genCounter work?

  • Upload a .txt file and genCounter will match the words in your file to the gendered language dictionary. It will then give the document a score based on the "genderedness" of the text in the document.

How was the gendered language dictionary developed?

  • Used Amazon's MTURK platform to crowdsource words that people perceived as masculine, feminine, or neutral that are common in politics.
  • Participants gave these scores to each word on a 1-7 scale (closer to 1 is feminine and closer to 7 is masculine).
  • As this was developed to examine political texts, this may not be appropriate to determine the "genderedness" of texts outside of the political domain.
